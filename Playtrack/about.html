About PlayTrack

Track, review, and revisit your movies, games, and books in one personal media journal.

Overview

What is PlayTrack?

PlayTrack is a personal media tracking system that helps you manage your films, games, and books in one place. You can easily log your entries, add ratings, write your thoughts, and even attach images.

Core idea

It brings your films, games, and books together into a clean personal archive that is easy to update and revisit.

Goal

Purpose of the System

To help users stay organized with their media activities and to keep track of their personal experiences. It allows users to document their thoughts, and keep track of what they've already explored.

Why it matters

A simple system makes reflection easier and turns random media use into something more intentional and memorable.

Solution

Problem Solved

PlayTrack solves the problem of scattered notes and forgotten titles by giving users one reliable place for logging and reviewing media.

Main benefit

Less time trying to remember titles, fewer scattered notes, and one organized place for your reviews and ratings.

Audience

Target Users

It is designed for students and young adults who want a simple and personalized system for managing their favorite media.

Best fit

Great for users who enjoy watching, playing, reading, and want a cleaner way to keep track of what they finished and how they felt about it.
