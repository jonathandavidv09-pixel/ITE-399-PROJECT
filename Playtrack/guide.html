What is PlayTrack?

A personal media tracking system designed to help users organize and reflect on the content they consume. Whether it's films, games, or books, PlayTrack allows you to keep everything in one place.

What You'll Learn

This guide will walk you through the system's features and show you how to use them effectively. By the end, you'll know how to log media, track your progress, and better understand your preferences.

Features

Everything you need to track your media journey.

  • User Profiles

    Create a personal account to keep your media logs private and organized. Each profile stores your entries, reviews, and stats securely.

  • Media Logging

    Add entries for movies, series, games, or books. Include details like title, type, genre, personal rating, notes, and even poster images.

  • Reviews and Ratings

    Quickly rate and write reviews for each media item using structured templates so you can remember your thoughts and experiences.

  • Library Management

    Easily view, search, filter, and sort your logged media by title, type, genre, or rating to stay organized and find entries quickly.

  • Dashboard and Personal Stats

    See an overview of your media habits with total items logged, average ratings, top genres, and other personal statistics.

  • Visual Summaries

    Track your habits visually with simple charts or tables that summarize your media consumption patterns over time.

How to Use PlayTrack

Follow these steps to get the most out of PlayTrack

  1. Create Your User Profile

    Sign up or log in to your personal account. Your profile stores all media logs, reviews, and personal stats.

  2. Check Your Dashboard

    The dashboard provides a quick overview of your activity, showing items logged per category and recent activities.

  3. Add Media Entries

    Click the "Add Media / Plus sign" button in the dashboard, enter details, and upload poster images.

  4. Write Reviews and Rate Media

    Open any media entry from your library, use the structured template, and save your review and rating.

  5. Manage Your Library

    Use search, filters, or sorting options to find items by title, type, or genre. Edit entries anytime.

  6. Explore the Summary Page

    View top genres, totals per category, top-rated entries, and average ratings in one place.

  7. Use Your Profile Page

    View your watchlist and favorites, and update your username, bio, or profile information.

  8. Adjust Settings

    Access the Settings page to see your basic information and change your password anytime.
